Autoplaylists

New autoplaylists will be synced once you create them.
Existing autoplaylists are synced periodically according to your sync period setting.
See the syncing FAQ for more details.

Uninstalling the extension will irrecoverably delete your autoplaylists.
To reset the extension, prefer reloading to uninstalling.
