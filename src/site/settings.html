Settings


The number of minutes to wait between syncing your autoplaylists with Google.
Lower numbers sync more often, keeping playlists more accurate at the cost of more ui refreshes, higher mobile battery use, and danger of problems on Google's end.
Setting this lower than 10 minutes is not recommended.
Set to zero to disable periodic syncing. You can still sync manually with the "sync now" button.
Regardless of this setting, changing an autoplaylist definition will always sync that playlist immediately.



Enabled by default, this splits large syncs into multiple smaller sync requests.
This spreads out load on Google, making syncs succeed more often at the cost of more ui refreshes.
You must manually reload the extension after changing this setting.
